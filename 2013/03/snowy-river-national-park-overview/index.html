Overview

  • 제목 : 힐링 캠프 (Pack Carry: Rodger River Gorge - Snowy River National Park)
  • 동행 : Melbourne Bushwalkers 총인원 14명 MBW.org.au

일정

  • Day 2 (Purple) : Moonkan Track
  • Day 3 (Blue) : Gorge Walk (Side Trip)
  • Day 4 (Red) : Rodger River Track
